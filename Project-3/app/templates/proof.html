Ratio of Feminine and Masculine Words by Industry

Frequency of Feminine Words by Industry


The results presented in this visual confirm our hypothesis that male dominated industries possess a higher ratio of masculine words in their job descriptions, and female dominated industries possess a higher ratio of feminine words. In this case, male dominated industries (Financial Services, Computer Software, Aviation & Aerospace, Construction, and Security & Investigations) had more masculine gendered words; with Financial Services and Computer Software showing the largest variance in the masculine vs. feminine ratio. Most female dominated industries (Human Resources, Design & Graphics, Veterinary, and Environmental Services) had a higher ratio of feminine words, with the exception of one industry (Hospital & Healthcare), who's masculine ratio is marginally higher than the feminine ratio.

This visual represents the frequency of feminine words within each industry. In this case, industries in the "female dominant" category skew towards the right, indicating that feminine gendered words are extremely prevalent in job descriptions within those industries. The frequency of feminine words in the Veterinary industry for example is 48% higher than the frequency of masculine words in that industry. On the other hand, the industries in the "male dominant" category skew towards the left, indicating that masculine gendered words predominate. The frequency of feminine words in Aviation & Aerospace for example is 24% less than the frequency of masculine words.
